About EcoSwap

Learn about our mission, vision, and the team behind India's largest sustainable swapping community.

Our Story

EcoSwap was founded in 2025 by Purva Morge, Kartika Chavan, and Neha Deore with a vision to transform consumer habits in India. As students passionate about sustainability, we recognized the need for a platform that makes eco-friendly living accessible and rewarding.

Our journey began as a college project and has evolved into a comprehensive platform that promotes circular economy principles. We believe that small changes in consumption habits can lead to significant environmental impact.

Today, EcoSwap is India's leading sustainable product exchange platform, connecting eco-conscious individuals across the country and reducing waste through innovative swapping mechanisms.

Our Mission

To promote sustainability, reduce waste, and encourage a circular economy through a convenient, accessible, and eco-conscious product exchange platform.

Our Vision

To create India's largest sustainable swapping community, transforming consumption habits nationwide and making sustainability the norm.

Our Growth Goals

We've set ambitious targets to expand our impact and reach more eco-conscious consumers.

Year 1

Launch & Initial Growth

Launch the EcoSwap platform and acquire 10,000 active users. Establish partnerships with local eco-brands and build a strong foundation for growth.

Year 2

National Expansion

Expand user base nationally and partner with 20+ eco-brands. Introduce new features and enhance the user experience based on feedback.

Year 3

Monetization & Sustainability

Introduce premium subscriptions and advertisements to achieve break-even. Reach 100,000 active users and become the leading sustainable swapping platform in India.
