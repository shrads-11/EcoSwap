Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about EcoSwap

How do I join EcoSwap?

Joining EcoSwap is simple! Just click on the "Sign Up" button on our website or download our mobile app from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store. Fill in your details, verify your email, and you're ready to start swapping sustainably.

Is EcoSwap available nationwide?

We're currently focused on major cities in India including Pune, Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, and Chennai. We're expanding rapidly and plan to be available nationwide by the end of Year 2. You can check our website for updates on new locations.
